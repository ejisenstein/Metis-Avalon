Rules for the Game

Welcome to Avalonine, this is a game of hidden loyalty. Players are either Loyal Servants of Arthur (good guys) or aligned with the Evil ways of Mordred. Good wins the game by successfully completing three quests. Evil wins if three Quests end in failure. Evil can also win by successfully identifying Merlin at the games end or if a Quest cannot be undertaken. Here are the instructions

  1. You need to log in with your username to start this game, click the login panel and follow the instructions
  2. The game will begin when the game master enters the correct password into the game status screen
  3. Once the game begins, you will be randomly given a role. Here are the roles as follows
    • Normal Good Character: The most common player in the game. You want Quests to succeed. You only know that you are a good player
    • Merlin: Good Character: Merlin is blessed and cursed with knowledge. Merlin knows who the evil characters are, he does not want to reveal himself though, if the assassin correctly guesses Merlin, then Evil will prevail
    • Normal Evil Character: Your goal is to prevent three quests from succeeding. You know the other Evil characters
